History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2015 as a small community meetup for local developers and technology enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into an international conference that attracts participants from around the world. Each year, TechCon has expanded its tracks, workshops, and exhibition areas to reflect the latest trends in software development, cloud computing, cybersecurity, and digital innovation.

From its early days in a single auditorium to today’s multi-day, multi-stage event, TechCon has always focused on bringing people together to learn, share, and experiment with emerging technologies.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon 2024 is to empower people to build a better future through technology. We believe that innovation should be inclusive, ethical, and sustainable. TechCon provides a platform where students, professionals, entrepreneurs, and educators can come together to exchange ideas and learn from one another.

Through keynotes, workshops, and hands-on labs, we aim to inspire attendees to:

  • Explore cutting-edge tools and frameworks.
  • Collaborate across disciplines and backgrounds.
  • Design solutions that are secure, accessible, and user-centered.
  • Lead responsibly in a rapidly changing digital world.

Notable Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has welcomed inspiring speakers who have shaped the technology landscape. Below are a few of the notable voices who have shared their knowledge on the TechCon stage.

Dr. Ada Lovelace – AI Researcher

Dr. Ada Lovelace is a leading researcher in artificial intelligence and machine learning. At TechCon 2019, she delivered a keynote on responsible AI, highlighting how transparency, fairness, and accountability must guide the design of intelligent systems.

Linus Torres – Open Source Advocate

Linus Torres is an open source contributor and community leader who has helped maintain several widely used developer tools. At TechCon 2020, he spoke about the importance of community-driven projects and how open collaboration accelerates innovation.

Grace Hopper-Jones – Cybersecurity Expert

Grace Hopper-Jones is a cybersecurity specialist focusing on secure software development and privacy by design. At TechCon 2022, she led a panel on protecting user data in cloud-native applications and shared practical strategies for building secure systems.
